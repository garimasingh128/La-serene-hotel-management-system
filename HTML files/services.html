Dont Miss out your Workout


Start running on the Technogym treadmill at The Vines Resort & Spa and you might find yourself out of breath, fast—and it's not just because you’re putting your running skills to the test. The outdoor gym floats above 1,500 acres of private vineyards in the Uco Valley set at the base of the Andes, giving athletic travelers some of the most breathtaking views in the region. The elevated fitness center also features a variety of other cardio and strength-training equipment, along with a massive climbing wall, to give guests a full-body workout. Looking to get up close with the landscape? Try the resort’s in-vineyard yoga classes, or hit the running trails that weave throughout the scenic property.
