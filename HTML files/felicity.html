Oasis for Well-travelled

Combining exquisite design, attentive service and exciting flavours,a heaven for travellers

Why come here??

Set against the lush hillside high above Acapulco Bay sits Felicity Hotel, an exclusive 40-acre beachside oasis, known for being one of the most romantic hotels in the world. This resort does arrivals right, as you are greeted with an ice-cold cocktail and escorted to your private casita via a signature white and pink jeep. Then follow the flower petal path through your spacious rooms to your oceanfront patio.You just cant resist the Felicity temptation.

Features

FELICITY BREAKFAST


Whether you fancy waking up to an all you can eat full English Felicity Breakfast, or a lighter continental selection, you’ll find all kinds of fresh food on offer in the mornings to help get your day off to the best start.

STYLISH SPACES


Style, form, beauty and function. That’s what you’ll find at La Serene Hotels. From the stunning Wine Tower in Stansted to the Dom Lounge at the Felicity Hotel our focus is all about enhancing the unique traveler-experience through elegant, sophisticated structures and spaces for business and leisure.

INSPIRATIONAL LOBBIES & PUBLIC SPACES


Lobby and public space areas make the initial impression on Felicity Hotel guests and are about much more than check-in and check-out. We have enhanced all Felicity common areas to further uplift the overall guest experience. Every social space, meetings and events area, food and beverage venue and wellness space has been given the same level of attention that you’ll experience during your stay at any Felicity hotel.

PET-FRIENDLY ROOMS


We understand that your pets are like family, so we’re happy to welcome dogs and cats in our designated pet-friendly rooms at most hotel locations. When you stay with us, you can rest easy knowing that you (and your travel buddy) will feel right at home.

ESPACIO HERCILIA


Espacio Hercilia located on the ground floor of Felicity,is the perfect setting for a casual, fresh and fun meeting point. It presents a delicious Mexican menu of breakfast and family meals. Choose to eat inside our beautiful “neighborhood cafeteria” or in our inner courtyard. It has Wifi service, TV screens and a variety of local food to go.

THE FELICITY HUB


In the heart of the hotel, the splendid conference rooms, private residences and enchanting gardens offer majestic and unforgettable settings.Marriages, receptions or business meetings : we can organise private and professional customized events just for you.
